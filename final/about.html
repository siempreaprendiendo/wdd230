Carlsbad, California

Carlsbad is a coastal city in the North County region of San Diego County, California, United States. The city is 87 miles (140 km) south of downtown Los Angeles and 35 miles (56 km) north of downtown San Diego. As of the 2020 census, the population of the city was 114,746. Carlsbad is a popular tourist destination and home to many businesses in the golf industry.

Bountiful Foods

Bountiful Foods is a family owned and operated business that serves Carlsbad California and the surrounding areas. It started as a goal to be healthier and eat more fruits and vegetables. Our love for fresh fruits and vegetables grew into a passion and a desire to make it easier for others to get fresh produce. We started out working from our home and soon grew big enough to have our own storefront right here in our hometown of Carlsbad. We sell fresh produce directly form local farmers in the area as well as getting some of our supply from national and international suppliers. We deliver and manage orders over the phone and via our website. Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options to the local population as well as tourists visiting the coastal areas of California.
